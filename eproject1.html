ECO-HERO




INTRODUCTION

       Eco-Hero is an App designed for kids and their parents. It promotes the environmental consciousness through interesting e-games. By games and environmental protection tasks, parents and kids can know which behaviors would be detrimental or beneficial to the Earth, the plant we are living by. Besides, kids can earn points by cooperation with parents for games and points can be redeemed for lovely pets, encouraging kids to play more and learn more.

       The first video is the whole process about how we design the app, including topic description, persona design, interview, etc. The second video is the display of our app, including the interface and the interaction.
