GARBAGE GO!



INTRODUCTION

       GARBAGE GO! aims to popularize environment-friendly life style and teach people how to dispose their rubbish in a eco-friendly methods. Specifically, App of GARBAGE GO! contains two parts. One is about sorting rubbish and the other, like Pinterest, provides people to display their handicrafts made by rubbish.


DISPLAY-SORT RUBBISH

  • Homepage

  • Funny Game
    • Game helping to know the rubbish classification
  • Systemic Knowledge
    • Quick search of the rubbish category


DISPLAY-HANDICRAFTS

  • News
    • The latest publish of the handicraft made by rubbish
  • Search
    • Quich search of different rubbish which has been made into handicrafts
  • User Management
    • Individual information, publish and collection
